ABOUT ME

Marcy Williams-Pudalov

CREATOR AND LEARNER EXTRAORDINAIRE

I have a passion for the arts in every branch; from fashion to the literary world, from photography and the fine arts to the culinary arena. I combine these passions with a love of treasures from past eras near and far, and am inundated with inspiration around every corner. Mining this rich surplus allows me to create with a nod to both the past and the future.

As a graduate of Savannah College of Art and Design, I was able to explore the art world, mining inspiration from all parts of the art and design world while adding to my knowledge.

Most recently, I have entered into the world of web design and development. I was working as a personal assistant at a law firm when their website crashed. I volunteered to figure out how to fix it. However, this required me to learn website development from the ground up, as I had absolutely no coding experience whatsoever. I ventured in on a path of self-directed learning, and I have never looked back. I was hooked on the development process and decided that code creation was what I truly wanted to do with my life.

It took me a while to figure out my career direction, but I am really glad I had the chance to find clarity and discover a career I'm truly passionate about

The best part of this? There is always something new to learn, so I could, in theory, quench my always present thirst for knowledge in this never ending stream.
