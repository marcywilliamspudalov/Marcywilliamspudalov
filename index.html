ABOUT ME

Marcy Williams-Pudalov

I have a passion for the arts in every branch; from fashion to the literary world, from photography and the fine arts to the culinary arena. I combine these passions with a love of treasures from past eras near and far, and am inundated with inspiration around every corner. Mining this rich surplus allows me to create with a nod to both the past and the future.

As a graduate of Savannah College of Art and Design's Fashion Department, I was able to explore the art world, mining inspiration while adding to my knowledge.

Most recently, I have entered into the world of web design and development. This is an exciting new area, and I am devouring knowledge in this new universe as quickly as I can wrap my brain around it!
